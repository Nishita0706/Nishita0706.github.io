Rainwater Harvesting
  • I had to create a play model, which involved designing a small-scale representation of how rainwater harvesting works.
  • The model demonstrated how rainwater can be collected, filtered, and stored for various uses, such as gardening or household purposes.
  • By building this model, I was able to show how simple techniques can help manage water sustainably and reduce waste.
  • Using renewable resources like rainwater to support eco-friendly practices.
  • It was a hands-on way to understand the importance of conserving water.
